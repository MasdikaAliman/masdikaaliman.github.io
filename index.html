About Me

Hello! My name is Masdika Aliman, and I am an undergraduate student majoring in Electrical Engineering with a focus on Robotics at Batam State Polytechnic. I am an active member of the Middle-Sized League (MSL) Soccer Robot Team at the Barelang Robotics and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (BRAIL), where I contribute to the development of advanced robotics systems, including localization, object detection, kinematics, control systems, and navigation systems. With a passion for innovation and problem-solving, I am enthusiastic about applying my skills to real-world technical challenges and collaborative projects.
